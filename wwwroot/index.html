How It Works

Smart Trash has the objective of increasing the efficiency of garbage collection paths, reducing the overall distance traveled and the amount of fuel consumed. We achieve this through the use of sensors that measure the amount of space that a bin has left, and creates a path according to these informations. The register of the bins, and the process of following the path will be achieved thanks to the Smart Trash app: "Clean Way" .
